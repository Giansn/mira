### Reference Document Analysis
- **Document**: MA02_LNW_Duygu_Karadag.pdf (FHNW Master thesis)
- **Purpose**: Extract FHNW citation style and formatting standards
- **Key Standards Identified**:
  1. **In-text citations**: `(vgl. Autor Jahr: Seite)` for paraphrases, `(Autor Jahr: Seite)` for direct quotes
  2. **Page ranges**: En dashes `139–141` (not hyphens)
  3. **Two pages**: `23f.` (no space)
  4. **Multiple authors**: `(Müller/Overbye 2010: 47)` or list format
  5. **Bibliography formatting**: Strict FHNW templates for monographs, journal articles, chapters, online sources
